Welcome to Picasso Exotic Aquatics

Welcome to Picasso Exotic AquaticsWelcome to Picasso Exotic AquaticsWelcome to Picasso Exotic Aquatics

Picasso Exotic Aquatics is working hard to serve both the dedicated hobbyist and the "living art" lover. With over 6,000 gallons of fresh and salt water aquariums on display in a unique gallery environment, no other aquarium store in America can offer the sensory experience . 

With over 50 years of combined aquatic experience, means you can enjoy a wide range of products, livestock, service and design brought to you in an atmosphere of complete professionalism. Whether you are looking for that rare fish, axolotl, or coral. that sets your aquarium apart from all others, or a peer review collaboration of your maintenance procedures.

You can feel confident that you are tapping into a wealth of technical knowledge and expertise.
