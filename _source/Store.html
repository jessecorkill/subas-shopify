We have the largest selection for Freshwater and Saltwater aquariums.

At Picasso Exotic Aquatics we directly import our animals directly from the collection sites. We feel that fish directly caught and sent to us directly are healthier, more colorlful and keep our prices low Vs. the traditional store. 


We currently have over 6000 gallons of holding capacity for Freshwater and Saltwater Fish.


For our Freshwater Enthusiast we stock Live plants from ADA, Tropica, and our own source from Malaysia. Both Tissue Cultures and Potted Plants. 

 

For our Saltwater Hobbyist we have 12 stock units filled with a wide variety to choose from. We get animals from Bali, Jakarta, Red Sea, Kenya, Etc. In addition we have 800 gallon Coral raceway filled with Coral and Frags. 


We stock many high end brand to start a new aquarium or outfit your existing one with the best brands. See our partners below.
